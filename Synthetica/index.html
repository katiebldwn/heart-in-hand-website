Sherri Buck Baldwin

Welcome! I create beautiful artwork for licensing on greeting cards, giftware, fabric, garden flags and more. Share your heart with my art!

Drawing has been my passion for as long as I can remember, beginning with private art lessons in various mediums throughout my childhood. I turned my passion into a career licensing my artwork for the past 26 years on a wide range of products from such companies as Legacy Publishing, Leanin' Tree, The Lang Companies, Evergreen, Custom Decor, Roman, Inc., and RAZ Imports among others. While I continue to pursue licensing I am also realizing my dream of producing a line of high-quality paper products featuring my hand-drawn artwork and input from my daughter Kate. I work in a variety of mediums, including ink, watercolor, colored pencils, soft pastels, and carved relief prints. My ideas grow from sketches to finished images in my home studio, Heart in Hand Ltd.
